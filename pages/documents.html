School Rules & Regulations

  • All students must wear complete and clean uniform during school hours.
  • Punctuality is expected for all classes and school activities.
  • Mobile phones are not permitted during class hours without permission.
  • Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students is mandatory.
  • School property must be preserved; any damage will attract a penalty.

Personal Needs & Stationery Requirements

📚 Basic Stationery

  • • 10 Exercise books (200 pages each)
  • • 5 Notebooks for rough work
  • • Mathematical set / Geometry box
  • • Pens (blue, black, red) and pencils
  • • Ruler, eraser, and sharpener

🏠 Boarding Personal Items

  • • Bed sheets, pillowcase, and blanket
  • • Towels and toiletries (soap, toothpaste, etc.)
  • • Mosquito net (recommended)
  • • Casual wear for weekends
  • • Padlock for personal cupboard
