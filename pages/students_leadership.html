Leadership Structure

An Organized Students' Leadership Body

An organized students' leadership body is essential for promoting order, unity, and effective communication within a school. It is made up of committed student leaders who work together in a structured system to support the administration and represent the interests of the student community.

Leadership Spotlight

Head Prefect

Our student leadership team works closely with teachers and administrators to maintain discipline, lead events, and support the wider student body. The Head Prefect serves as the primary liaison between students and school administration.

  • Coordinates all prefect activities
  • Chairs student council meetings
  • Represents students at school events
