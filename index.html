Welcome to Hopeful Future SS, Kayunga

Hopeful Future Secondary School is a high-quality private secondary school founded in 2019. It is a mixed boarding school offering both Arts and Sciences at "O" and "A" Level. The College is owned by experienced and well-educated individuals with a strong commitment to excellence in academics and post-school life.
